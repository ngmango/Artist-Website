Will Smith Fan Page

Will Smith was born on September 25th 1968, in West Philadelphia, where he was raised. He first came into the spotlight in the late 80's with his friend and collaborator, DJ Jazzy Jeff. However, it wasn't until 1990, when Will starred in the hit sitcom The Fresh Prince, where Will's fame really took off. Smith started his solo career in the mid 90's, with his hit song "Men in Black" from the motion picture of the same name, which Will also starred in. Will has gone on to star in many more Blockbusters over the last 20 years, becoming one of the biggest movie stars of our generation, and one of the most bankable actors ever.
